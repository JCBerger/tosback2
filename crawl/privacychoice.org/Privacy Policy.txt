Take these steps if you have a problem.
<ul>
<li> Restart your browser <p> A fresh session makes sure that your browser is up to date.
If your tabs don't automatically re-load, you can find them in Settings &gt.
History.
Note that PrivacyFix does not work if you are using Private or Incognito browsing.
</p>
</li>
<li> Disable add-ons <p> Other add-ons (particularly privacy tools) can interfere with PrivacyFix.
Go to Addons (Firefox) or Extensions (Chrome) to disable them temporarily and re-run PrivacyFix.
